Question: Simplify the following expression:   \[2x+3x^2+1-(6-2x-3x^2).\]
3o-mini: To simplify the expression 2x + 3x² + 1 - (6 - 2x - 3x²), first distribute the negative sign through the parentheses:

  2x + 3x² + 1 - 6 + 2x + 3x²

Now, combine like terms:
• For x²: 3x² + 3x² = 6x²
• For x: 2x + 2x = 4x
• Constant terms: 1 - 6 = -5

So, the simplified expression is 6x² + 4x - 5.
