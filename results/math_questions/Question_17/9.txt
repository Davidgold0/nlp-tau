Question: Compute_x000D_
\[\frac{\tan^2 20^\circ - \sin^2 20^\circ}{\tan^2 20^\circ \sin^2 20^\circ}.\]
3o-mini: To compute the expression

  [ (tan²20° − sin²20°) / (tan²20° · sin²20°) ],

we begin by writing tan²20° in terms of sin and cos:

  tan²20° = sin²20° / cos²20°.

Substitute this into the numerator and denominator:

Numerator:
  tan²20° − sin²20° = (sin²20° / cos²20°) − sin²20°  
    = sin²20° (1/cos²20° − 1)  
    = sin²20° ((1 − cos²20°)/cos²20°).

Denominator:
  tan²20° · sin²20° = (sin²20° / cos²20°) · sin²20°  
    = sin⁴20° / cos²20°.

Now, the expression becomes:

  [sin²20° ((1 − cos²20°)/cos²20°)] / [sin⁴20° / cos²20°].

Since cos²20° appears in both the numerator and the denominator, we can cancel it:

  = (sin²20° (1 − cos²20°)) / sin⁴20°  
  = (1 − cos²20°) / sin²20°.

Recall the Pythagorean identity:
  sin²θ + cos²θ = 1 ⟹ 1 − cos²θ = sin²θ.

Thus:

  = sin²20° / sin²20°  
  = 1.

So, the value of the given expression is 1.
