Question: Simplify the following expression:   \[2x+3x^2+1-(6-2x-3x^2).\]
3o-mini: First, distribute the negative sign through the parentheses:
  2x + 3x² + 1 − 6 + 2x + 3x²

Now, combine like terms:

• For the x² terms: 3x² + 3x² = 6x²  
• For the x terms: 2x + 2x = 4x  
• For the constant terms: 1 − 6 = -5

So, the simplified expression is:
  6x² + 4x - 5
